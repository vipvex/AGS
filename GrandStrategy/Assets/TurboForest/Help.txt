
Turbo Forest getting started:

1. Create new scene
2. Create "3d objects" -> "Plane"
3. Scale plane by 100,100,100
4. Create "3d object" -> "Turbo forest"
5. Drag and drop your plane to TF component's distribute objects list
6. Press "Generate" button in TF component

For more info look at http://goo.gl/gqxhqT
